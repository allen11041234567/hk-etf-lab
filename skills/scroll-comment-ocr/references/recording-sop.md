# Recording SOP for fast comment extraction

## Goal

Produce a screen recording that is easy for OCR to scan within a few minutes.

## Best practices

- Keep the comment text large and centered.
- Scroll in one direction only.
- Pause about 0.5–1.0 seconds per screen.
- Avoid sudden fast flicks.
- Avoid keyboards, notifications, and overlays.
- If replies matter, record them in a second pass.
- Prefer one topic/thread per video.

## Best two-video pattern

1. Video A: main comments only, sweep top to bottom
2. Video B: expanded replies only, focused补录

## Why this helps

- Less OCR noise
- Fewer repeated frames
- Easier chunking
- Faster raw dump delivery
